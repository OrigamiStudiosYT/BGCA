GREAT FUTURES START HERE.

Massachusetts Clubs give youth a head start on a great future.

We believe every kid has what it takes. Every child deserves a safe, positive, and inclusive environment, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, gender expression, sexual orientation, ability, socio-economic status, and religion. Our goal is to enable the Boys & Girls Clubs in Massachusetts to provide a Club experience that assures that success is within reach of every young person who enters our doors, with all members on track to graduate from high school with a plan for the future, demonstrating good character and citizenship, and living a healthy lifestyle. Across the nation, Boys & Girls Clubs are making it happen every day. Nationally, 97% of members expect to graduate from high school, rivaling the graduation rates of the best high schools in the nation even though Boys & Girls Club members often face many hardships. Club involvement also improves youth physical fitness, mental outlook, and community involvement. We do everything we can to empower and support Massachusetts youth, so they can live their best lives.

During these trying times, we strive to keep you updated on what's important.
To this end, we have disabled extraneous pages to keep things simple in this time of complexity.




The Massachusetts Alliance of Boys & Girls Clubs is a group of 40 independent Boys & Girls Club corporations that operate throughout the state of Massachusetts. The Alliance was formed in 2000 for the purpose of seeking and sharing financial and program opportunities to help better serve the children of our state.
