We give your youth a head start on a great future in all our Clubs

Our goal is to help every member to achieve their greatest potential. Through our afterschool programs, we keep America's youth off the streets and help them through their hardships. 76% of Club members in 5th through 12th grades who attend the Club at least once per week are on track for high school graduation. Regularly attending Club members in eighth, 10th and 12th grades are more likely to volunteer at least once a month than their peers nationally. In a Harris Interactive survey, 54% of Boys & Girls Club alumni said the Club “saved my life.”

The Massachusetts Alliance of Boys & Girls Clubs is a group of 40 independent Boys & Girls Club corporations that operate throughout the state of Massachusetts. The Alliance was formed in 2000 for the purpose of seeking and sharing financial and program opportunities to help better serve the children of our state.
